Details
  • Prizes: Prizes will awarded on the following basis:
    • 1st – 5th Place Teams – Prizes/Gift Cards
    • 10+ additional prizes will be given to randomly selected participants
  • | Eligibility: Any high school student residing in the state of Utah may participate.
  • Registration: Registration must be completed by both students (at this link) and teachers/coaches (at this link).
    • Participants must register by midnight on Saturday, April 27th, 2024.
  • Computer Systems: Teams of up to 3 students will need to provide their own laptop (just 1) with which they will access the contest platform via the internet.
  • Contest Platform: The contest will be conducted on HackerRank. The HackerRank website can be accessed by most common operating systems (including Linux, MacOS and Windows) and supports common languages (including but not limited to Java, Python3 and C++).

    Teams are encouraged to practice on the HackerRank platform (create an account as a “Developer”) ahead of the contest so as to be familiar with how it works. Specifically, we encourage students to complete at least a few easy problems on the Algorithms and/or Data Structures parts of the HackerRank Problem Solving section, so that they will be comfortable with the platform. Another really good platform for additional problem solving practice is Kattis (this is the one that BYU’s competitive programming team generally practices on).
  • Spectators: Family and friends are invited to watch through the contest leaderboard. The leaderboard will be published closer to the contest date.
